Salmon and Roquefort pasta

This is thje best recipe you will ever make

Ingredients

  • 350g pasta (Spaghetti, linguine ...)
  • 2 salmon pavers
  • 100 g of smoked salmon
  • 20 cl of fresh cream
  • 100 g of Roquefort cheese
  • sunflower seeds

Directions

  1. Steam salmon breasts, crumble them roughly
  2. Cut the smoked salmon into small pieces
  3. Roast the sunflower seeds in a frying pan without fat
  4. Heat the fresh cream, add the Roquefort cheese and melt it
  5. Boil water for pasta, and cook as directed in instructions
  6. Drain the pasta and add the cream to Roquefort cheese, mix well
  7. Spread on plates, sprinkle with salmon, smoked salmon and sunflower seeds
  8. Serve immediately
